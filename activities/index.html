Research interests

My research interests are at the intersection between theoretical aspects of statistical signal processing, machine learning and their applications to remote sensing applications.

From my Ph.d where I worked on change detection for time series of SAR images, I have interested myself as well to hyperspectral images, GPR signals and EEG measurements.
